Tangled

The magical tale of the girl with 70 feet of golden hair

Overview

Tangled is a 2010 American 3D computer-animated musical fantasy film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. Loosely based on the German fairy tale "Rapunzel" in the collection of folk tales published by the Brothers Grimm, it is the 50th Disney animated feature film.

Key Information

  • Directed by: Nathan Greno and Byron Howard
  • Release date: November 24, 2010
  • Running time: 100 minutes
  • Genre: Animation, Adventure, Comedy
  • Music by: Alan Menken

Plot Summary

The film tells the story of Rapunzel, a lost young princess with magical long blonde hair who yearns to leave her secluded tower. She accepts the aid of a handsome intruder, Flynn Rider, to take her out into the world which she has never seen. Together, they embark on an exciting journey filled with adventure, romance, and self-discovery, while being pursued by Rapunzel's manipulative "mother" Gothel who wants to keep Rapunzel's power for herself.

Voice Cast

  • Mandy Moore as Rapunzel
  • Zachary Levi as Flynn Rider/Eugene Fitzherbert
  • Donna Murphy as Mother Gothel
  • Ron Perlman as the Stabbington Brothers
  • Jeffrey Tambor as Big Nose Thug
  • Brad Garrett as Hook Hand Thug

Main Characters

  • Rapunzel: A spirited and determined young woman with 70 feet of magical, golden hair
  • Flynn Rider: A charming bandit who becomes Rapunzel's unlikely companion
  • Mother Gothel: Rapunzel's manipulative "mother" who uses the magic in Rapunzel's hair to stay young
  • Pascal: Rapunzel's loyal chameleon companion
  • Maximus: A determined palace horse who relentlessly pursues Flynn
