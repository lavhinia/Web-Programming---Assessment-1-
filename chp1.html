HOMEMADE HAWAIIAN PIZZA RECIPE

A perfect blend of sweet and savory, this homemade pineapple pizza features a crispy golden crust, tangy tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and juicy pineapple chunks. Add ham or bacon for a classic Hawaiian touch, or keep it simple for a fresh and tropical twist.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet 2tsp active yeast
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 3 cup of warm water
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 1tspn salt

  • 1 tbsp olive oil (for brushing the crust)

Methods to follow

Step 1: Prepare the dough

  1. In a small bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Mix until dough forms, then knead on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp towel, and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Step 2: Shape the Pizza

  1. Preheat oven to 475¬∞F (245¬∞C).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Transfer to a pizza stone or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Brush the edges with olive oil for a golden crust.

Step 3: Assemble the Pizza

  1. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  2. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously.
  3. Add pineapple chunks and ham (or bacon if using).
  4. Sprinkle oregano and red pepper flakes for extra flavor.

Step 4: Bake

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
